# Weather/Terrain Inject Cards

## How to Use
These cards modify environmental conditions. Apply during TLP Step 1 (for planning) or during execution (for dynamic weather changes). Update the weather section or provide as a weather update.

---

## Card WT-01: Heavy Rain and Reduced Visibility
**Apply During:** TLP Step 1 or during execution

**Inject Text:**
"Weather Update: Heavy thunderstorms have moved into the area. Visibility reduced to 100 meters. Terrain is becoming muddy, affecting vehicle movement. Plan for wet weather operations."

**Effect:** Candidate must adjust movement plan, visibility considerations, and terrain analysis.

---
