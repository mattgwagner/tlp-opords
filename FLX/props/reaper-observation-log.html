REAPER Watch Team 3 — Facility Log

28 FEB
Arrived at the facility. Government soldiers departed two days ago. Buildings empty. Established watch from the building on the west side with good view of the main north-south road. Reported to Cdr Kavan — facility is unoccupied and usable.
01 MAR — 0630
confirm with supply team Quiet night. No vehicle or foot movement on road. Checked all buildings — some equipment left behind by government forces. Tables, chairs, generator (not working). Storage area east of facility has covered containers, could be useful for supply cache. Reported to higher.
01 MAR — 1430
Two vehicles drove north on main road past the facility, did not stop. Civilian type. No military markings. Could not see passengers clearly from this distance. Entered in report for commander.
02 MAR — 0800
Moved watch position. The western building has best observation of both the road and the facility entrance. Can see vehicles approaching from north or south on the main road. The junction where the two main roads cross to the north is visible on clear days. Ahmad says we should move closer — staying here is safer, we can observe without being seen.
03 MAR — 0700
Foot patrol from our watch team walked the western forest road south. No activity. Trail is clear and concealed — good route for messengers. Resupply team used the main road yesterday, came from the south with food and batteries. Next resupply in 2 days.
03 MAR — 1600
! report immediately Military-type vehicles observed near the large complex to the north (the training area). Multiple vehicles, personnel in uniform. Too far to count but more activity than usual. Could be routine or could be preparation. Reported to Cdr Kavan. He says continue to observe and report any movement toward the facility.
04 MAR — 0900
More activity at the northern complex. Foot traffic visible on the road between their facility and our position. They have not come south of the road junction yet. Kavan sent word — if military forces move toward the facility, withdraw to the crossroads junction and report. Do not engage unless engaged.
